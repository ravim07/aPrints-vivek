Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find a answer? Contact us

What are the minimum resources needed to create an awesome magazine?

All that is needed is eager content contributors from your community and one individual (student, teacher or parent) who is familiar with graphics design tools such as Adobe Creative Cloud and aPrintis can help with graphics design if the community doesn’t have a single individual with graphics expertise.

What graphics software should we use?

Adobe Cloud that includes Adobe Creative Suite, Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator is used by the vast majority of communities. However. some have used Microsoft Publisher as well.

What page counts do we print?

We use offset printing for orders sizes of 1000 and more and digital printing for order sizes less than 1000. For offset printing the page counts that we print are 16, 24, 32 and 48. For digital printing, we print page counts of 8, 12, 16, 20, 24, 28, 32 and 48. If you have a need for a different page count and order size, please let us know and we will try to find you a solution that works for you.

What is the minimum quantity that you order?

We offer only those formats that we believe are standard and which we can provide publishers with a great price and quality proposition. Since aPrintis is focused on serving small communities rather than individual on-demand orders, the minimum order quantity that we serve is 100.

What is the typical turnaround?

Our turnaround to any location in mainland US is 7 calendar days after the job has been approved for print - barring uncontrollable weather delays or other emergency/catastrophes. Often, the job is delivered a day or two before then.

How long does it take to get a job approved for print

Depending on the print-readiness, a typical job might require an iteration or two before it is deemed ready to print.

What paper stock does aPrintis use?

70# for offset and 80# for digital.

What are the most common elements that need to changed for print readiness?

Bleed on every page, fonts that haven't been embedded in the document, image resolutions tend to be the most common issues with initial submission of jobs.

Can we do other formats?

As mentioned earlier, we focus on common formats and order sizes. However, even if our ad rate sheet doesn’t include your desired option, please let us know and we can see if we can still get you a good offer.

Does aPrintis operate outside the US?

No. aPrintis operates in mainland US - not in Hawaii, Puerto Rico, Alaska.
